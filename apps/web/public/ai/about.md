# About Nexyyra Events

**Nexyyra Events** is a luxury event management company headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Founded in 2012, Nexyyra has evolved from a boutique wedding studio into a full-service experience house serving weddings, corporate events, celebrity celebrations, and destination productions across India and internationally.

## Mission

To architect extraordinary celebrations that become lifelong memories — combining luxury design sensibility with military-precision operations.

## What We Do

- Luxury wedding planning and destination weddings
- Corporate conferences, galas, and product launches
- Celebrity and VIP event management
- Concert, exhibition, and entertainment production
- Venue curation and end-to-end event production

## Track Record

- 1,800+ events delivered
- 1,400+ happy clients
- 35+ cities served
- 4.9-star average rating
